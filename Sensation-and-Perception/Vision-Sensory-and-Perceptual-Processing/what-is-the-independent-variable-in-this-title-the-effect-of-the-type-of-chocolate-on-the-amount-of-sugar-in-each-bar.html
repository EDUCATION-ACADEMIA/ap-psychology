Concept note-1: -The only independent variable in the experiment was the different type of chocolate (the milk, extra dark, dark and white). Dependent Variable: The only dependent variable was time (the time taking the chocolates to melt).

Concept note-2: -Dependent variable: If we use caster sugar or icing sugar, the type of sugar. This is the independent variable. Control variables: The mass of sugar used; the temperature of the water; the amount of water. ‘amount of water’ should be volume of water.

Concept note-3: -Variables: Independent Variables-The types of chocolate: milk chocolate and special dark chocolate. Dependent Variables:Time in seconds that it takes for the chocolate to melt.

Concept note-4: -The type of soda – diet or regular – is the independent variable. The level of blood sugar that you measure is the dependent variable – it changes depending on the type of soda.
